What started out as a simple coffee shop idea, turned into an ideal of values I hope to see in future shops and restaurants.

The very first sketches and ideations played around with lots of basic coffee symbols, most prominently is the cup; which soon evolved to include the intials “SB” for Steep | Brew.

The name Steep | Brew is an encompassing form of an ideal shopowner who is both a coffee and a tea connoisseur with the beliefs of a sustainable future, which many small business owners should (or even must) now consider if they want to cast a wide net to millenials and the generation after who are concerned with the environment and human carbon footprint. We live in an ever-changing world, and this brand project hopes to reflect the ongoing look of the future.
